# 🌱 Community Service Project

### Agriculture and Its Impact on Farmers

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on understanding **agriculture and the challenges faced by farmers** in rural areas. It was conducted as part of the Community Service Project (CSP) under B.Tech (CSE) program.

The study was carried out in **Katrenipadu village, Konaseema District, Andhra Pradesh**, where real-time interaction with farmers helped identify key socio-economic and agricultural issues. 

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the **living conditions of farmers**
* Identify **major agricultural problems**
* Provide **practical solutions and awareness**
* Develop **social responsibility and communication skills**
* Apply academic knowledge to real-world scenarios

---

## 🏡 About the Community

* 📍 Location: Katrenipadu Village, Andhra Pradesh
* 👨‍🌾 Major Occupation: Agriculture
* 🌾 Main Crops: Paddy & Vegetables
* 👥 Population: ~4000+ people
* 📊 Literacy Rate: ~72.5% 

---

## 🔍 Activities Performed

* Conducted **field surveys with farmers**
* Collected data on:

  * Land size
  * Income
  * Crop types
  * Problems faced
* Identified key issues
* Conducted **awareness campaigns**
* Suggested **solutions and improvements**

---

## ⚠️ Problems Identified

* ❌ Not getting minimum price for crops
* ❌ Pest attacks on crops
* ❌ Irrigation and water issues
* ❌ Natural disasters (floods, heavy rains)
* ❌ Delay in payments from government
* ❌ Lack of healthcare facilities

---

## 💡 Solutions Proposed

### Short-Term Solutions

* 📱 Use of **Plantix App** for pest detection
* 💧 Water storage methods
* 🌿 Use of **seaweed extracts** for better yield
* 🚿 Adoption of **drip irrigation**

### Long-Term Solutions

* Government intervention for **fair crop pricing**
* Improved irrigation infrastructure
* Awareness programs for farmers
* Better support from agricultural authorities

---

## 📢 Awareness Campaign

* Conducted campaigns in village areas
* Explained problems & solutions to farmers
* Demonstrated use of technology (Plantix app)
* Encouraged modern and eco-friendly farming methods
